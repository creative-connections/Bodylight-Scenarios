e-Golem is digital simulation-based textbook with interactive advanced animation for learning about human physiology. Currently only selected themes of human physiology and pathophysiology is covered. See the section bellow. Available topics and scenarios physiology of hemodynamics physiology and pathophysiology of iron metabolism physiology of acid base balance

E-Golem comes in web version. iOS and Android version will come soon for mobile devices and tablets.
